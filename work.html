My work includes...


The UCL Audio Tour was created to offer staff, students and the public a way to find out more about the Bloomsbury campus and those who studied at UCL.

The project involved producing, recording and editing the tracks themselves, as well as writing script for narration by Jonathan Dimbleby, and the development of a separate mobile app that uses a phone's GPS to interact with the tour. I taught myself JavaScript to develop the app using AppFurnace, tested it, collected user feedback and released it on the app stores.
